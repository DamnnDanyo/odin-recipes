Brownies

Description

These rich, fudgy brownies are topped with a decadent homemade frosting!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on the stove.
  2. Remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients.
  3. Beat in the dry ingredients.
  4. Spread the batter in the pan and bake.
